
# Underwater Adventure - Episode I: A New Beginning 

## 🎬 Opening Crawl

### EPISODE I: A NEW BEGINNING

It is an age of mystery beneath the waves. Ancient kingdoms of coral and stone lie scattered across the ocean floor, their secrets long forgotten by the surface world.

Rumors spread through the deep that a powerful relic — the Heart of the Tides — has been discovered in the ruins of a sunken civilization. It is said to hold the power to control the currents themselves.
Now, rival explorers and creatures of the deep race through dark trenches and glowing reefs to find it first.

Guided only by courage and curiosity, a young diver begins a journey into the unknown… one that could change the fate of the oceans forever.
